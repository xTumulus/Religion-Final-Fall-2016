The purpose of this website is to gather information surrounding the book of Abraham: it’s origins, it’s doctrinal offerings, what scholars both LDS and non-LDS have said regarding its historicity, and brief explanations of the basic people and places surrounding the book’s origin.

Our purpose is not to create an exhaustive compilation of this information or a scholarly analysis of the book of Abraham and its origins, rather it is to be used simply as a resource for finding reliable information and to create a good starting place for honest seekers of the truth to begin their own search. We aim to provide brief summaries of available information, written from a perspective of faith in the divine calling of Joseph Smith and of the scripture he brought to light.
