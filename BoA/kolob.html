Kolob

Often seen as a very peculiar object in the uniquely Mormon cannon, kolob is the star nearest to the throne of God. While much speculation has been generated around kolob and its literal meaning/location, the most agreed upon interpretation of these kolob is as an analogy for Christ and his position nearest to the God the Father as being on the right-hand of God.
